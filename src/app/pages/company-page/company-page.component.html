The Future of Audio Synthesis
Entropy Audio is a company focused on research, AI, and sound.
Our mission is to bring a new paradigm of tools to composers.
We enable composers to create sound with language. This lowers the technical barrier and cost of obtaining high quality sound for the less experienced, and augments creativity for all.
Our state of the art machine learning systems understand music theory and sound design. Users comfortable with the technical side of creating music will feel right at home.
"Music is as much of a science as it is an art".
